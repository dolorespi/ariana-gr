Albums

Positions

Positions is the sixth studio album by American singer Ariana Grande. It was released by Republic Records on October 30, 2020. Grande created the album with focus on her vocals, while its subject matter conveyed emotional healing

Thank U, Next

Thank U, Next is the fifth studio album by American singer Ariana Grande, released on February 8, 2019, by Republic Records, conceived in the midst of Grande's personal struggles, including the death of ex-boyfriend Mac Miller and the end of her engagement to Pete Davidson.

Sweetener

Sweetener is the fourth studio album by American singer Ariana Grande. It was released on August 17, 2018, through Republic Records. The album explores themes of romance, sexual intimacy, unhealthy relationships, womanhood, anxiety, and perseverance through hardships

Dangerous Woman

Dangerous Woman is the third studio album by American singer Ariana Grande. It was released through Republic Records on May 20, 2016. Dangerous Woman takes incorporations from numerous musical genres, including dance, disco, house, trap, reggae and electropop. Dangerous Woman was released to positive reception from music critics, who praised Grande's vocal prowess, matured lyrical content and her adaptation to different musical styles.

My Everything

My Everything is the second studio album by American singer Ariana Grande, released on August 25, 2014, by Republic Records. Primarily a pop and R&B record, My Everything expands on the 1990s retro-R&B style of its predecessor, while also approaching new genres such as EDM, electropop and dance-pop.

Yours Truly

Yours Truly is the debut studio album by American singer Ariana Grande. It was released on August 30, 2013, by Republic Records. Musically, Yours Truly is a pop and R&B record, while incorporating 1990s hip hop, 1950s piano pop, and doo-wop music within its production
